# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 19, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the "MDX NB" application ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") accesses, uses, and protects information when used to manage advertising campaigns on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram).

The App is an internal tool operated by Madox ("the Operator") for the purpose of paid media management on behalf of consenting clients. It is **not a public-facing application** and is not intended for end-user (consumer) signups.

## Information We Access

When authorized by an ad account owner (Operator or Operator's client), the App may access:

- Ad account metadata (account ID, name, currency, time zone)
- Campaign, ad set, and ad configuration data
- Advertising performance metrics and insights (impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, etc.)
- Page-level information for pages explicitly granted to the App
- Custom audience metadata (not the underlying user lists)

The App does **not** collect data from end users of Facebook or Instagram. The App does not access personal user profiles, messages, friend lists, or any consumer-side data.

## How We Use Information

Accessed information is used solely for:

- Generating performance reports and dashboards for ad account owners
- Optimizing and managing advertising campaigns on behalf of clients
- Producing month-to-date and period-over-period performance summaries

We do not sell, rent, or share accessed information with third parties.

## Data Storage and Retention

Data accessed via the Meta Marketing API is processed for reporting purposes and stored only as long as necessary to deliver services to the relevant ad account owner. Aggregated reporting outputs (e.g., PDF or PowerPoint reports) may be retained per client agreements.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect accessed information, including secure storage of API credentials and access tokens.

## Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the Meta Marketing API. Use of Meta's platforms is governed by Meta's own terms and policies, available at https://www.facebook.com/policies.

## Data Deletion

To request deletion of any data associated with your ad account that the App has accessed, please email **madoxads@gmail.com** with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your ad account ID. We will respond within 30 days.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
